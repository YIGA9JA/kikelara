What we collect

We collect information you provide and basic data needed to run the website and fulfill orders.

Information you provide

  • Name, email, phone number
  • Delivery address
  • Order details and messages you send us

Information collected automatically

  • Device and browser details
  • Pages viewed and actions (like adding to cart)
  • IP address and approximate location

How we use your information

To provide services

  • Process orders and deliveries
  • Customer support and updates
  • Prevent fraud and keep our site secure

To improve the experience

  • Fix issues and improve performance
  • Understand what products people love
  • Personalize your shopping experience

Sharing your information

We do not sell your personal information. We only share it when needed to operate the store.

Service providers

Shipping partners, payment processors, and tools that help run the site — only as needed.

Legal or safety reasons

If required by law, or to protect customers, the public, or our business.

Cookies & tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to remember your preferences, keep your cart working, and understand site usage.

Cookie types we use
  • Essential: required for cart and checkout.
  • Performance: helps us understand site performance.
  • Preference: remembers settings and choices.
Your choices

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Blocking some cookies may affect how the site works.

Data retention

We keep information only as long as needed for orders, support, and legal or accounting requirements.

Security

We use reasonable safeguards to protect your data. No system is 100% secure, so please use strong passwords and keep your account information safe.

Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or request a copy of your data.

Requests

To make a privacy request, contact us and include the email/phone you used for your order.
